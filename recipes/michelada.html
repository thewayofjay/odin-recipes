Michelada

Recipe Description

Michelada is a mexican beer cocktail that is light, savory, refreshing with a dash of hot sauce.

Ingredients

  • Tomato juice (or Clamato juice)
  • Lime juice
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Hot Sauce (tabasco, tapatio, cholula)
  • Mexican lager beer
  • Tajin Seasoning
  • salt

    • Directions

    1. Salt the rim: Run a lime wedge across the rim of your beer glass and dip in salt and tajin.
    2. Add seasonings: Add tomato or Clamato juice, lime juice, worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, and hot sauce.
    3. Add beer: top with your favorite mexican beer. Stir, taste, and adjust seasonings if desired.
