Pan-Seared Steak

Recipe Description

A classic and simple recipe to make your carnivorous side satisfied.

Ingredients

  • New York Steak, Top Sirloin, or Ribeye Steak
  • Butter
  • Garlic cloves
  • Rosemary
  • Oil

    • Directions

    1. Pat dry- use paper towels to apt the steaks dry to get a perfect sear and reduce oil splatter.
    2. Season generously- just before cooking steaks, sprinkle both sides liberally with salt and pepper.
    3. Preheat the pan- on medium and brush with oil. Using just 1/2 Tbsp oil reduces splatter.
    4. Sear steaks- add steaks and sear each side 3-4 minutes until a brown crust has formed then use tongs to tunr steaks on their sides and sear edges (1 min per edge).
    5. Add butter and aromatics- melt in butter with quartered garlic cloves and rosemary sprigs. Tilt pan to spoon garlic butter over steaks and cook to your desired doneness. (see chart below)
    6. Remove steak and rest 5 minutes before slicing against the grain.
    Steak Doneness Temp Chart:
    • Medium Rare:145 degrees F
    • Medium:160 degrees F
    • Well Done: 170 degrees F
