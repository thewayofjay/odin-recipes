Aguachile Sabrosos!

Recipe Description

Aguachile is an authentic spicy mexican seafood dish that is guaranteed to make your taste buds water and spice up!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound raw shrimp
  • 1-2 large limes
  • generous pinch of salt
  • 1/4 red onion (very thinly sliced)
  • splash of white vinegar
  • 1 cup sliced cucumber
  • avocado slices
  • Tortilla chips

    • Aguachile Marinade

    • 1/2 cup of fresh lime juice
    • 1 garlic clove
    • 1 cup cilantro
    • 2 jalapenos
    • 1 serrano chili
    • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

      • Directions

      1. Combine lime juice, chiles, cilantro, white vinegar, and 1 teaspoon of salt in a blender; until smooth.
      2. Divide shrimp onto 2 shallow plates
      3. Pour chile-lime mixture evenly over the 2 plates of shrimp. Place in the refrigerator and marinade until the shrimp turn opaque and white, 15 to 25 minutes.
      4. Top each plate with red onion, avocado. Place cucumber around the edges. Sprinkle with salt.
      5. Enjoy! (be careful it's spicy!)
